What is AI Jailbreaking?

AI jailbreaking is the practice of testing AI models to discover security weaknesses, vulnerabilities, and unsafe behaviors. Shehan Nilukshan and the AI RedCell team conduct ethical AI penetration testing to strengthen AI systems and ensure safe public deployment.
