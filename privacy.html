Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

AI RedCell ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website airedcell.dev and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, company name, and other contact information you provide through our contact forms.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Course Progress: If you use our learning platform, we may track your course progress and completion status locally in your browser.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Process your security assessment requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you relevant updates about our services (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information:

  • FormSubmit: We use FormSubmit.co to process contact form submissions. Please review their privacy policy.
  • GitHub: Some of our resources link to GitHub repositories.
  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts for typography.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies and local storage for functionality purposes, such as saving your course progress. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
